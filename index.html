How it works

01

Draft your team

Pick five houseguests at the start of the season. Signing up late? You'll get a smaller roster to keep things fair, and your scoring starts from the next eviction.

02

Earn points

Every comp win, every survived eviction, and every finale bonus adds to your team's score. The longer your picks last, the more they're worth.

03

Climb the leaderboard

Compete with friends across the whole season. Get push notifications when big moves happen and see exactly which players are racking up the most points.

+N: each survived eviction earns points equal to that eviction number. Survive #1 → +1, survive #2 → +2, and so on. The longer they last, the bigger each survival is worth.
